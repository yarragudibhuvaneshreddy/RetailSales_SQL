## ðŸ”Ž Business Problems Solved

This project addresses the following real-world business questions using SQL:

1. Retrieve all sales transactions made on **November 5, 2022**.

2. Identify transactions where the product category is **'Clothing'** and the quantity sold is greater than 10 during **November 2022**.

3. Calculate the **total sales revenue generated by each product category**.

4. Determine the **average age of customers** who purchased items from the **'Beauty'** category.

5. Identify all transactions where the **total sales amount exceeds 1000**.

6. Analyze the **total number of transactions by gender within each product category**.

7. Calculate the **average monthly sales** and determine the **best-performing month in each year**.

8. Identify the **top 5 customers** based on the highest total purchase value.

9. Determine the **number of unique customers per product category**.

10. Categorize transactions into **shifts (Morning, Afternoon, Evening)** based on purchase time and calculate the **number of orders per shift**.

## ðŸ§  Sample Query

```sql
-- Retrieve Clothing transactions with quantity > 10 in November 2022

SELECT *
FROM retail_sales
WHERE category = 'Clothing'
  AND quantity > 10
  AND sale_date >= '2022-11-01'
  AND sale_date < '2022-12-01';
```
